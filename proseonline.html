What is a pro se motion for an online hearing?

A pro se motion means you are asking the immigration judge for something without a lawyer. In this situation, you are asking the judge to allow you to attend your hearing online instead of in person.

Warning: If you do not ask properly and miss court, the case can move forward without you.

Step-by-step: how to file the motion

Step 1 — Get your case details

  • Name
  • A-number
  • Court location
  • Hearing date and time

Step 2 — Write the motion

Your motion should clearly say:

  • You are requesting permission to appear online
  • You are representing yourself
  • You are trying to avoid missing court
  • Why appearing in person is difficult

Step 3 — Add proof

Include documents that support your reason. Judges are more likely to grant requests that have real evidence.

Step 4 — File before it's too late

File early. Waiting until the last minute is one of the biggest reasons these motions fail.

Step 5 — Do NOT assume it's granted

Until the judge approves it, you still need to treat your hearing as in-person.

Key point: Filing the motion is what protects you — not waiting.

What to include in your motion

  • Clear request for online hearing
  • Reason with explanation
  • Supporting documents
  • Statement you want to comply with the court

Common mistakes

  • Waiting too long
  • No proof
  • Assuming online is automatic
  • Missing hearing anyway
